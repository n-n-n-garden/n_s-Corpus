If some other dude
Treated me like you
What would you do
To him? 

Would you sit him down
Explain the error of his ways
Counsel him kindly and wisely

Or would you consider setting
Him ablaze
Toes first
Work through
His family
Until he was the only one left.

If some dude
Treated me like
I was less than garbage
Because even garbage has a schedule
Even garbage
Requires notification 
To cancel

Would you shrug your shoulders
Or ruminate
His demise?

Thank fuck
I am more peaceful
Than you. 

The worst thing I will do
Is live
And be there
The next time
You need me. 

Not in spite of you
Not because of you
But solely
Because
I want to.

And that is something 
You can anticipate about me
But never
Understand.
